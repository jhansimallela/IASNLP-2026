# Authors Guidelines
Participants will have to submit a technical report for their project (written in ACL anthology format) which will be subject to selection by reviewers. U

## Submission guidelines
The submission must adhere to the formatting in the submission templates, which is available as an Overleaf template.

Submissions are via Google Forms. A submission may be desk rejected if it does not adhere to the submission template requirements. To help the authors avoid desk rejections, we compiled a list of common problems to check before submission.

Submissions will also be rejected if they do not adhere to the reviewing schedule:

## Review schedule:
